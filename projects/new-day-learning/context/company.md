# Company Information

## Mission
Empowering Independence — supporting adults with special needs through quality DDD services.

## Organization
- **Company:** New Day Learning LLC
- **Industry:** DDD Services (New Jersey)
- **Location:** New Jersey

## Team Structure
- Owner/Admin — full operational control
- Billing Admin — invoicing and payments
- Support Coordinators — client-facing, read-only portal access
- Staff / Providers — session delivery
- Accountant — financial reporting, read-only

## Target Audience
- **Primary Users:** Owner/Admin, Billing Admin
- **Secondary Users:** Support Coordinators, Staff, Accountant
- **End Beneficiaries:** Adults with special needs (DDD clients)
- **Tech Skill Level:** Basic
- **Device Split:** 70% Mobile, 30% Desktop

## Legal / Compliance Notes
- DDD (Division of Developmental Disabilities) regulated services
- Full audit trail required for all actions
- Document retention minimum: 3 years
- RBAC with field-level restrictions where necessary
